About us

ThakurBari Mandir is a Hindu temple, built in 1915 by Village people of Falka such as Baudhu Sah, Bhagi Rath Maharaj, Mahavir Seth, Raghu Nath Sah, Vishwnath Ram and some other people also.
Initially Dhanurdhari thakur was the pujari of this temple after that Nage Padit, Gopi pandit, Manoj Pandit.
In Other occasion like Ramnavmi and Ganesh puja there are extra pandit ji, Nunu Jha and Suman Jha.
Property(Farms or Dharamshala) of Thakurbari is surveillance of ThakurBari Mandir NavNirman Committee.
